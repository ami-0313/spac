Salamba Performing Art Centre

Founded in 2005, Salamba Performing Art Centre has been guided by Isuru Weerasinghe (Artistic Director) and Geethani Warnapura (Artistic Directress) from its very beginning.

Our logo symbolizes the dancing feet and the traditional ornament called “Silambu”, the anklet worn when performing classical Sri Lankan dance. We are registered as a Dance Foundation under the Department of Cultural Affairs, Ministry of Cultural Affairs, Sri Lanka (Registered No: CD/03/08/01/06/CO 245).

Our vision is to carry Sri Lankan dance forward to future generations and to the world while enhancing both traditional and modern Sri Lankan dance forms. To achieve this, we conduct dance classes, workshops, lectures, and performances in multicultural festivals, universities, and other related venues.

Our dance group consists of well-educated professional dancers and drummers with extensive experience. We provide opportunities for students to gain practical experience and express their artistic talents.

Dance is our passion. It is how we move, how we walk, how we speak, and how we express ourselves. Dance is our life.
